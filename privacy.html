1. Information We Collect

We collect information to provide better services to our users. The types of information we gather include:

  • Personal Information: This includes details such as your name, email address, phone number, and any other information you provide when filling out forms or interacting with our services.
  • Non-Personal Data: We may collect data like your IP address, browser type, operating system, and browsing behavior on our website to help us improve your experience.
  • Cookies and Tracking Technologies: Small files stored on your device to help us remember your preferences and gather statistical data.

2. How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used for the following purposes:

  • To provide, operate, and maintain our services.
  • To respond to your inquiries, support requests, or feedback.
  • To improve our website functionality, content, and overall user experience.
  • To send promotional or informational emails, which you can opt out of at any time.
  • To comply with legal obligations or enforce our terms of service.

3. How We Protect Your Information

We take the protection of your data seriously and have implemented various security measures, including:

  • Secure server environments to store and process your information.
  • Encryption technologies to safeguard sensitive data during transmission.
  • Regular system updates and monitoring to prevent vulnerabilities and potential threats.

Despite these measures, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is completely secure. Therefore, while we strive to protect your data, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

4. Sharing Your Information

We respect your privacy and will never sell your personal information. We may share your data under the following circumstances:

  • With trusted third-party partners who assist us in operating our website and providing services, provided they agree to keep your information confidential.
  • To comply with legal obligations, enforce our policies, or respond to legal requests.
  • In case of a business merger, acquisition, or sale, where your information may be transferred to the new entity.

5. Your Rights

You have the following rights concerning your personal information:

  • Access: Request access to the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correction: Update or correct any inaccuracies in your data.
  • Deletion: Request that we delete your personal information, subject to legal or contractual obligations.
  • Opt-Out: Unsubscribe from marketing emails or adjust your cookie preferences.

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@coohstoryhub.com.

6. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Cookies are small files stored on your device that help us remember your preferences, analyze trends, and improve our services. You can manage your cookie preferences by adjusting your browser settings. Note that disabling cookies may affect the functionality of certain features on our website.

7. Updates to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect changes in our practices or for legal and regulatory reasons. Any updates will be posted on this page with the effective date at the top. We encourage you to review this page regularly to stay informed about how we protect your information.
